RetroMesh
Community-driven social darknet — no AI, no algorithms

Restoring a human-first, corporate‑free social web.

RetroMesh is a community-driven darknet project recreating a 2010s-style social web: curated nodes, link directories, forums, and small communities. Access is via a RetroMesh fork of Tor Browser. Privacy features are available but optional.
